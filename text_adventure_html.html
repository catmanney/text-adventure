How to Play:
-To navigate, type: "north","east","south","west","up" or "down"
-To take an item, type: take "item" (take pencil, take eyeglass-case, etc)
-To use an item, type: use "item-one" on "item-two" (use cat-food on cat, use bottle on river, etc)
-To check inventory, type: inventory
-To check room for items, type: examine
-To get help, type: help (or simply scroll to the top of the page)
